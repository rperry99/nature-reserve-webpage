Attractions

Nature Trails

Come see our beautiful tails. They are fun for the whole family! For the kids, there are short, paved trails. For the more experienced hikers, there are long and steep trails.

Visit the Animals

At Baum, we take in sick and injured animals and nurture them back to health with the ultimate goal to put them back in the wild. You can visit some of our animals that we are currently helping!

Events

Releasing Dabi the Raccoon
September 21st

We have been nurturing Dabi the male raccoon ever since we found him abandonded as a baby. Now, he is finally strong enough to be released back into the wild. There will be activities for the children such as face paintings, arts and crafts, and a presentation about raccoons by our resident wildlife expert, Adam Smith.

When we release Dabi, everyone can watch from the balcony at our nature reserve building. We will be relasing Dabi at 10am on September 21st, 2018.

Admission is free. Make sure you are here at least 15 minutes before 10am to make sure you can see Dabi being released! We will be doing all other activites all day.

Ghost Stories by the Fire
October 1st

We will have some spooky ghost stories to tell by the camp fire light. These are all true stories about our local area that are said to be haunted. Our speakers are the Haunted Housewives, local ghost hunters to the Northeast Ohio area. You will hear stories about places around Northeast Ohio that are said to have some ghostly visitors. Some of the places you will hear about include Punderson Manor, Willoughby Coal, and the Downtown Willouhby Cemetary, just to name a few.

Stories will begin at 7pm on October 1st, 2018. We will meet in the lobby of the Reserve Building and everyone will walk to our fire pit about 100 feet into the woods. We will also be serving hot chocolate and cookies for those of you who are interested.

Admission will be $5 per person at the door. All ages are welcome.

Play Life-sized Candyland
November 15th - December 1st

Everyone remembers the classic board game Candyland! We set up a life-size verison where you are the player pieces. You can play the actual game or you can just walk through the board enjoying the sights. There is also a small playgorund for the little children to play on.

Candyland will be open during our normal operating hours. If you would like to play, come visit us at the information desk and we will give you the deck of cards to play!

Admission is free so bring the whole family!

Holiday Light Walk
December 3rd - December 30th

We will have a little walk set up inside of our Reserve Building. It will be set up with a holiday theme. The walk will take about 10 minutes to complete. We will supply glasses for everyone that will make all of the lights on the walk twinkle like starts. You can also keep the glasses as a souvenier!

The admission is free and will be operating during our regular operating hours.
